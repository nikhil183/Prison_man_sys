Description About The Lawyer

The Lawyer is the one who will keep all records of his/her client(prisoner) from the Prison Management System.He will have his own unique ID for accessing the records,but he won't be able to do any kind of alterations in the data entries of the prisoner.He can only view all prisoners.

The Lawyer will not only keep track of the records of the prisoners but will also be in touch of his/her family for many leagal setups.As a Lawyer he/she will have direct connection with the court itelf and will keep all the updates regarding his/her client from the court.
