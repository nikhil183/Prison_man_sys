Jailor's Functions

The Jailor is the main entity of the Prison Management System.He will keep a track on all of the records related to every prisoner.He is the one who would have access of the database also and have access of the prisoner's data.Jailor will have two options, first he could see all the existing prisoners information and second can add new prisoner by providing unique ID to it.He will have all data of prisoner like personal iformation, the username,id,case related all data and parole information apart from tis he can make changes in the prisoner's information as per need.
